Be a part of the Enkryptiaa'18

ABOUT ENKRYPTIAA

ENKRYPTIAA'18, Government Polytechnic Nagpur is one of the largest college fests in Nagpur. Embodying the true spirit of youth, Enkryptiaa provides a platform for young talent to showcase their varied skills.

About Campus Ambassador

Now, you too have a chance to become a part of this technical extravaganza. With Campus Ambassador Program you get a chance to be an extended part of the organising team of Enkryptiaa’18. All you have to do is lead the contingent from your college taking part in Enkryptiaa and make sure that people in your institute know about it. Be the leader in a crowd of followers.

Why Campus Ambassador?

An opportunity to improve your communication and managerial skills by interacting with people coming from diverse fields.
An opportunity to improve your leadership skills.
Be the face of Enkryptiaa’18 in your college.

Campus Ambassador Responsibilities

PUBLICISE- our events in your college via different means.
COORDINATE- with us and help us in your college.
SOCIAL MEDIA- publicize Enkryptiaa in your college using the different social networking platforms.
TASKS- that are assigned to you must be completed before the deadline.

Incentives

* A CERTIFICATE OF APPRECIATION AUTHENTICATING YOUR WORK AS A CA.

* A LETTER OF RECOMMENDATION BY THE CHAIRMAN OF ENKRYPTIAA’18.

Eligibility

Everyone is eligible for this post, irrespective of the year you are in. All we need is your enthusiasm and zeal to come up with fresh ideas to help us make this edition of Enkryptiaa splendorous.
